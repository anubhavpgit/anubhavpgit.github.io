Hi, I am Anubhab Patnaik, a CS engineer from India. Interested in math, science, and engineering, I work with software engineering and try to solve real-world problems. I maintain a blog where I publish my thoughts and ideas. I write about tech, science, and my life. Here's what I am upto these days.

I work with whatever piques my interest. I used to dabble in ML and now work with web, mobile, and CMD applications. I polish my DS and algo on Leetcode. This site and the blog are built using my custom SSG, zuzu. Lately, I've been working on a custom bit-torrent client to download files using TypeScript, and I plan on porting it to Rust. Meanwhile, I am also exploring esoteric programming languages and will be working on a brainfuck interpreter.

Whether it's geeky, nerdy, or creative, I'm always up for it!
